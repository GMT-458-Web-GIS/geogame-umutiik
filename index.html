Flag Guessing Game

Welcome to the Flag Guessing Game! Test your knowledge of the world's most populous countries by identifying their flags. Click on the correct country pin for each flag to earn points. You have three attempts for each flag, with decreasing points for each incorrect guess.

How to Play

Welcome to the Flag Guessing Game! Here's how you play:

  • A flag of a country will be displayed.
  • Your task is to click on the correct country on the map.
  • You have 3 attempts for each flag. The fewer attempts, the more points you score.
  • Use the "Hint" button if needed, but it will cost you 5 points.
  • Score as many points as possible before time runs out!
